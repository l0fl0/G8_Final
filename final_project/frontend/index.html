Welcome

GameVault lets you create an account, explore a game catalog, submit ratings and written reviews, keep track of favorites, follow other reviewers, and discover new games through personalized recommendations.

Main Features

  • Create an account and sign in
  • Browse games by genre and tags
  • Read and submit ratings and reviews
  • Add games to your favorites list
  • Follow other users and see what they play
  • View personalized recommendation sections
